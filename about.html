About

Hi, I’m Aakshi. I’m a designer and researcher working at the intersection of sound, media, and digital systems. I’m interested in how people experience technology and culture, and how research and design can come together to create thoughtful, human-centered work.

I graduated from Minerva University with a BA in History, where I built a strong foundation in research, critical thinking, and storytelling. I’m currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Marketing Communication Research, focusing on UX research, market research, and media studies, with a particular interest in music and audio platforms.

Outside of work, I enjoy singing, cooking, and organizing open mics with friends. I’m drawn to slow media, field recording, and sound-based storytelling, and these influences often shape how I approach my projects.
